Services Built Around Your Business Needs

Three core service areas designed to handle the operational work that takes your focus away from growth. Every service we offer is customizable to fit your industry, team size, and budget.

Core Service

Customer Service Center Operations

This is our core offering. We build and manage dedicated customer service teams that represent your brand to your customers across all communication channels.

Inbound Call Handling

Your customers call, our agents answer with your brand greeting and resolve their issues directly.

Outbound Call Campaigns

Follow-up calls, satisfaction surveys, appointment confirmations, and proactive customer outreach.

Live Chat Support

Real-time chat support on your website and apps. Our agents handle multiple conversations simultaneously.

Email & Ticket Management

We process and respond to customer emails and support tickets within your defined SLA timelines.

Complaint Resolution

Our agents follow your escalation procedures to resolve complaints quickly and maintain customer satisfaction.

After-Sales Support

Order tracking, return processing, warranty claims, and post-purchase follow-up for your customers.

Real Example

We manage the full customer support operation for an e-commerce client processing over 500 orders daily. Our 12-person team handles all inbound calls, live chat, and return processing, maintaining a 94% customer satisfaction score.

Administrative Services

Office Administrative Support

Administrative tasks consume hours every week. We take that work off your team so they can focus on strategic priorities that drive revenue.

Data Entry & Processing

Accurate and fast data input for your databases, CRM systems, and internal platforms.

Document Management

Organization, filing, and management of business documents in both digital and physical formats.

Scheduling & Calendar Management

Meeting coordination, appointment scheduling, and team calendar management.

Records & Filing

Structured and easily accessible business record maintenance for compliance and operations.

Report Preparation

Regular report compilation, data presentation, and operational summaries for your leadership team.

General Office Coordination

Daily office activity coordination including vendor management and procurement support.

Real Example

A logistics startup uses our admin services to process an average of 200 orders per day, manage delivery scheduling, and handle all operational documentation. As a result, they avoided hiring three full-time back-office staff and saved approximately 40% of their admin operational costs.

IT Services

IT Service & Technical Support

Businesses that rely on technology need responsive tech support. We provide help desk and IT service management without the cost of building a full in-house IT department.

Help Desk Support (Tier 1 & Tier 2)

Our team handles technical inquiries, password resets, and basic to intermediate troubleshooting.

Technical Troubleshooting

Diagnosis and resolution of technical issues across software, hardware, and network systems.

System Monitoring & Alerts

24/7 system monitoring with real-time notifications when anomalies or downtime occur.

Software Installation & Configuration

Setup and configuration of business applications, productivity tools, and specialized software.

User Account Management

User account management, access rights, new employee IT onboarding, and offboarding.

IT Asset Tracking

Company IT asset tracking and recording including laptops, servers, software licenses, and other devices.

Real Example

A SaaS company with 150 employees uses our help desk services to handle an average of 45 internal support tickets per day. Our team resolves 90% of Tier 1 tickets within 30 minutes, allowing their in-house IT team to focus on infrastructure development and strategic projects.
